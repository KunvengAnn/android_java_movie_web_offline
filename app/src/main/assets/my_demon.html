Korea: My Demon

Overview

Korea: My Demon is a captivating South Korean supernatural thriller film. The movie delves into the world of dark folklore and ancient legends. Directed by Lee Director, the film features a talented ensemble cast including Song Joong-ki, Kim Soo-hyun, and Song Kang.

Plot

In Korea: My Demon, a young archaeologist discovers an ancient relic that unleashes a malevolent force. As the demon wreaks havoc across the land, a group of unlikely heroes, including Song Kang's character, must band together to unravel the mystery behind the demon's origins and find a way to stop its reign of terror before it consumes everything they hold dear.
