Iron Man 4

Overview

Iron Man 4 is an upcoming superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Iron Man. It is the fourth installment in the Iron Man film series and serves as a sequel to Iron Man 3. The movie is directed by John Director and stars Robert Downey Jr. reprising his role as Tony Stark / Iron Man.

Plot

The plot details for Iron Man 4 are currently undisclosed. However, it is expected to continue Tony Stark's story and explore new challenges and adventures as Iron Man battles against powerful enemies and protects the world with his advanced suit and technology.
