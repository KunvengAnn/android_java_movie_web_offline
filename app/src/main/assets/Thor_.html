Thor: New

Overview

Thor: New is the latest installment in the Thor film series, part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). The movie follows the adventures of the Asgardian superhero Thor, the God of Thunder. Directed by Jane Director, the film stars Chris Hemsworth reprising his role as Thor.

Plot

In Thor: New, the story continues after the events of the previous films. Thor embarks on a new quest to protect the Nine Realms and maintain peace across the universe. He faces formidable challenges, confronts powerful villains, and explores his own inner journey to discover the true meaning of being a hero.
